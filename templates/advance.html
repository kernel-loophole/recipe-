6-Week Flytright Program:

Week 1:

Day 1:

  • Jump Rope (Speed) - 3 sets of 1 minute
  • FlytX Pushup Complex - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • FlytX Squat Complex - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • FlytX Inverted Calve Raise Complex - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Pull-Up - 5 sets of til failure
  • Box Jump - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Full Crunch - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • FlytX Sprint Complex - 3 sets of 15 yards (Full Speed)
  • FlytX Crunch Complex - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • FlytX Lunge Complex - 3 sets of 10-12 reps (each leg)
